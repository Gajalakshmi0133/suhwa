Help & Support

Master Suhwa with our comprehensive guides, interactive tutorials, and AI-powered support.

Knowledge Base

  1. Register: Create your account using the sign-up form.
  2. Login: Access your personal dashboard securely.
  3. Explore: Start using our detection tools immediately.

Our real-time engine processes video frames to generate instant subtitles. For best results:

  • Maintain bright, even lighting.
  • Ensure your hands are fully visible in the frame.
  • Use a neutral, non-distracting background.

Upload pre-recorded videos for high-accuracy translation.

Supports MP4, AVI, MOV up to 200MB.

How accurate is the system?

Accuracy exceeds 95% in ideal conditions but varies with lighting and camera quality.

Which languages are supported?

We currently support ASL, ISL, BSL, and regional languages like Tamil, Hindi, Malayalam, and Telugu via translation.

Master sign language with our interactive AI-powered lessons.

  • Learning Mode: Step-by-step guides for alphabets and common words.
  • Practice Mode: Real-time feedback on your signing accuracy.
  • Revision: Revisit signs you've struggled with in the past.

Manage your identity and security settings:

  • Avatar: Change your profile picture or use our preset characters.
  • Security: Update your password regularly in the settings panel.
  • Preferences: Set your default sign language (ASL, ISL, etc.).

Communicate directly with others using integrated translation:

  • Start a Call: Generate a unique link to share with others.
  • Live Captions: See sign language translated into text during the call.
  • Screen Sharing: Share your workspace while maintaining the translation window.

Your data is yours. We provide tools to manage and export it:

  • Export History: Download your detection history as a .txt file.
  • Data Portability: Export your account information in .sql format.
  • Clear Logs: Wipe your local detection and landmark logs at any time.

If you're experiencing lag or detection errors:

  • Browser: Ensure you are using the latest version of Chrome or Edge.
  • Hardware: Enable 'Hardware Acceleration' in your browser settings.
  • Lighting: Avoid backlighting; ensure your face and hands are well-lit from the front.
