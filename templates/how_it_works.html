The Core Workflow

Three main stages that power our real-time recognition engine.

01

Visual Capture

High-speed frame processing from your webcam at 30 FPS. Our engine optimizes lighting and contrast automatically to ensure reliable landmark tracking even in challenging environments.

02

Kinetic Mapping

Using advanced pose estimation, we map 21 key points on each hand. This skeletal data captures precise movements, finger rotations, and hand orientation without storing your personal video data.

03

Neural Inference

The skeletal coordinates are fed into our custom-trained Neural Network. Within milliseconds, the model analyzes the sequence and predicts the sign with high statistical confidence.
