About YOLOv11 Detection

This mode uses a **YOLOv11 (You Only Look Once)** deep learning model to detect ASL signs directly from the image pixels. Unlike the standard mode which tracks hand landmarks, YOLO detects the hand and the sign simultaneously.

Bounding boxes and confidence scores are rendered server-side.
Performance Tips
  • Keep hand fully in view
  • Ensure good lighting
  • Stay 3-5 feet from camera
