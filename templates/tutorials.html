Tutorial: The Letter 'A'

Beginner Module
CATEGORY Fingerspelling
Step-by-Step Execution
  1. Close your hand into a loose fist.
  2. Rest your thumb against the side of your index finger.
  3. Keep your palm facing forward towards the observer.
  4. Hold the position firmly for 1 second.
CONTEXTUAL USAGE

Used in fingerspelling names or acronyms. The foundation of many compound signs in ASL.
