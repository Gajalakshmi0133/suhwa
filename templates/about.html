Our Mission

To leverage advanced computer vision and deep learning to provide real-time, accurate sign language recognition that facilitates seamless interaction for everyone, everywhere.

Our Vision

A world where communication barriers are completely eliminated, making every social and professional interaction fully inclusive and accessible to the deaf community.

Our Journey

From a research concept to a global accessibility platform.

2019: The Spark

Initial research project started by a group of passionate developers aiming to solve daily communication challenges for the deaf.

2021: Prototype Launch

First working model released, capable of recognizing basic ASL alphabets with 80% accuracy in controlled environments.

2023: Real-time Evolution

Integration with MediaPipe and TensorFlow Lite, enabling high-speed, real-time recognition on web and mobile browsers.

Today: Global Impact

Supporting multiple sign languages and serving thousands of users globally in education and healthcare sectors.
